It’s 11:45 pm on election day and I haven’t looked at the results yet.

Well, that’s not entirely true. I checked about two hours ago and was not met with the kind of feedback I was hoping for. To be clear, I’m supporting Joe Biden on this one. And when I checked two hours ago, it was a much closer race than basically every single poll had predicted going into this thing.

I could say I don’t know how anyone supports Trump, but we’ve been past that for a while now. There’s clearly a lot about this nation, and about people in general, that I don’t understand.

Regardless… we’re going to get what we deserve, one way or another.
